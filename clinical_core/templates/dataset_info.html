Transparency and clinical background of the data powering DiaHealth AI.

Core Metadata

Dataset Name: Early Stage Diabetes Risk Prediction Dataset
Research Source: Sylhet Diabetes Hospital, Sylhet, Bangladesh
Publishing University: Metropolitan University, Sylhet, Bangladesh
Repository Host: UCI Machine Learning Repository (UC Irvine)
Funding: National Science Foundation (NSF)
Citation: Islam, M. M. F., et al. (2019).

Clinical Description

This dataset contains the signs and symptoms data of newly diabetic or potentially diabetic patients. It was collected using direct questionnaires from patients at the Sylhet Diabetes Hospital and was approved by medical professionals.

The model uses this data to identify patterns in early-stage symptoms, allowing for proactive health management.
